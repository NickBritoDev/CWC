007 Cassino Royale

James Bond's (Daniel Craig) 1st mission as Agent 007 takes him to Madagascar. His task is to spy on terrorist Mollaka (Sebastien Foucan), but not everything goes as planned. Bond decides to spy on the rest of the terrorist cell on his own, which leads him to the Bahamas. There he meets Alex Dimitrios (Simon Abkarian) and his girlfriend Solange (Caterina Murino). Alex is involved with Le Chiffre (Mads Mikkelsen), the banker of terrorist organizations spread across the planet, who intends to make money in a millionaire poker game in Montenegro, at Casino Royale. MI6 sends Bond to play against Le Chiffre, knowing that if Le Chiffre loses the match it will dismantle his organization. But for this task, agent 007 will have the company of the seductive Vesper Lynd (Eva Green), sent by M (Judi Dench) to accompany him on the mission.
