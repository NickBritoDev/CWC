Eternals

Eternals are super-endowed beings with characteristics such as immortality and manipulation of cosmic energy, and they are the fruit of the failed experiments of their own creator, the Celestial Arishem, since the creation of Earth millions of years ago. Destined to save the world and the human race from the Deviants, beings also created by the Celestial, the Eternals then defeat such beings and go their separate ways, hoping their creator will return with good news. But after centuries and millennia of waiting for him, the band of immortal heroes must now prepare for a new threat, and they must fight the Deviants again to ensure humanity's safety. Many internal conflicts can arise, between the love they feel for the Earth and the need to protect it above all, and the faith in what is above them. Each one must fight for what they believe in, and defend what is most important. Eternals takes place shortly after the events of Avengers: Endgame (2019), within the MCU universe, inspired by the comics, adapted by Marvel Studios.
